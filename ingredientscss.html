White Sauce Pasta

A timeless classic, White Sauce Pasta offers a perfect balance of rich, creamy textures and delicate flavors...

Ingredients for White Sauce Pasta



Ingredient Quantity Description
Pasta (Penne/Fettuccine) 200g Any pasta of your choice, typically penne or fettuccine.
Butter 2 tablespoons Unsalted butter for a smooth start to the sauce.
All-purpose Flour 1 tablespoon To thicken the sauce and create a roux.
Milk 1 cup Acts as the base for a smooth white sauce.
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves Adds a mild, savory flavor to the sauce.
Cheese (grated) 1/4 cup Parmesan, mozzarella, or cheddar for cheesy flavor.
Salt To taste Add mild heat and balance the creamy texture.
Oregano 1/2 teaspoon Dried Oregano for a touch of herb flavour.
Black Pepper (ground) 1/4 teaspoon Adds a mild spiciness and enhances the overall flavor.
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon Optional, to coat the pasta and add a hint of richness.
Vegetables (optional) 1 cup (chopped) Bell peppers, mushrooms, broccoli, or zucchini for additional texture and nutrition.
Chili Flakes 1/4 teaspoon Optional, for a touch of heat.
Parsley (fresh) 1 tablespoon (chopped) Optional garnish to add freshness and color.
